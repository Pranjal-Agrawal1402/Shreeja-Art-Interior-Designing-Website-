Our Story

Shreeja Art was founded in 2026 by Sandeep Pandey after a decade of leading interiors at large practices — and a growing conviction that the field had traded intimacy for efficiency. The studio began with a single commission and one principle: every space deserves to be understood before it is designed.

Today, we are a team of twelve designers, architects, and makers working across four continents. We do not have a signature style. We have a process that reliably produces work that feels, to the people who inhabit it, like it could not have been any other way.
